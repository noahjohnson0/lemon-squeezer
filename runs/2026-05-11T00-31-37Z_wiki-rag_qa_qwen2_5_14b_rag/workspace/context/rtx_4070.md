# GeForce RTX 4070

The GeForce RTX 4070 is a graphics card by NVIDIA, launched on April 13, 2023. Built on the AD104 graphics processor of the Ada Lovelace architecture, the RTX 4070 features 5888 CUDA cores. The card has 12 GB of GDDR6X memory on a 192-bit memory bus. The memory bandwidth is 504.2 GB/s.

The RTX 4070 has a base clock of 1920 MHz and a boost clock of 2475 MHz. The card has a Total Graphics Power (TGP) of 200 W and uses a 16-pin power connector (12VHPWR), with an adapter to two 8-pin connectors included in the box. The recommended PSU is 650 W.

The card supports DisplayPort 1.4a and HDMI 2.1a. It supports DLSS 3 with Frame Generation, ray tracing, and AV1 hardware encoding. The launch MSRP was $599 USD. The Founders Edition uses NVIDIA's signature dual-axial flow-through cooler with two fans, in a two-slot form factor measuring 244 mm in length.

For local LLM inference, the 12 GB VRAM comfortably fits 7B models at FP16, 14B models at Q4_K_M, and 30B mixture-of-experts models with their experts paged. Larger dense 30B+ models require offloading to system RAM and run substantially slower as a result.
